Blue Cross Blue Shield: Positive Language Training

Practice identifying and using positive customer service language in a healthcare call center context.

Why Positive Framing Matters

  • Creates better member experience and higher satisfaction
  • Reduces escalations and complaints
  • Builds trust and rapport
  • Increases efficiency in resolving issues

Part 5: Cultural Considerations

In the U.S., customer service language emphasizes empathy, empowerment, and clarity. Example phrases:

  • "I'm happy to help with that"
  • "Thank you for your patience"
  • "Let me take care of that for you"
  • "I'll find that answer for you"

Quick Reference: Positive Alternatives

  • Instead of: "That's not our policy" → Say: "Our policy is designed to..."
  • Instead of: "You have to..." → Say: "We recommend..."
  • Instead of: "You didn’t understand" → Say: "Let me explain that another way"
  • Instead of: "No, we don’t do that" → Say: "Here’s what we can do for you"
